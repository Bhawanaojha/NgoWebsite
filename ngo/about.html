ABOUT US

PRASHAST - THE HELP LIFE “PRASHAST” means the one who paves the way for another. It is an organisation created to improve the life and quality of the underprivileged in India. We have been working since 2009, but formally established in 2019 to provide better resources to the society. PRASHAST has collaborated with Delhi State Legal Services Authorities (DSLSA) and East Delhi Legal Services Authorities (EDLSA) for raising awareness and to provide free legal aid to the weaker section of the society. It is to ensure that no citizen is denied justice and every individual has an equal access to court irrespective of their economic status or disability.

MISSION

Our mission is to understand and support the underprivileged in various aspects of life. We provide them with the basic amenities which help them to achieve their fullest potential.

VISION

To create a positive change in the life of every individual and to save the underprivileged from further exploitation by creating awareness.

FUTURE FOCUS

Justice, Revivalism and rejuvenating them for new life. Descent and better living to their families. To provide support to extraordinary talented people.

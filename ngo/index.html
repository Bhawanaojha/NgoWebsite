Prashast
-The Help Life

An NGO in which we believe a warm smile is the universal language of kindness! We work to understand and empathise with the underprivileged and support them for a positive change.

Aims & Objectives

  • To empower citizens to create a positive social change.
  • To make people aware of the importance of education, governmental provisions and their rights.
  • To provide free and affordable medical facilities with special importance on hygiene and clean environment.
  • To provide easy access to vocational training and job opportunities.
  • To create a sustainable structure focusing on the holistic development of the underprivileged.
